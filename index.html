Midnight Crisis

Welcome to Midnight Crisis, a bone-chilling horror game that will leave you breathless. In this game, you play as Subject IV, a person who has been kidnapped by an evil pharmaceutical company to be used as a test subject in their twisted experiments. As Subject IV, you must navigate through the pharmaceutical company's complex, uncovering the truth of their sinister experiments while the facility is under lockdown.
